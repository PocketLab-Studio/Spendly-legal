# Privacy Policy for Spendly

**Last Updated: December 2, 2025**

PocketLab Studio ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Spendly ("the App") handles your information.

## Information We Collect

**We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.** All your financial data stays on your device.

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following information locally on your device:
- Transaction records (amounts, dates, categories, descriptions)
- Budget information
- Savings goals
- Recurring expense data
- App preferences and settings
- Premium subscription status

### Camera Permission

**Why we need it:** To scan receipts and automatically extract transaction information.

**How we use it:**
- When you choose to use the receipt scanning feature, the App accesses your camera to capture receipt images
- Images are processed to extract merchant name, amount, date, and items
- **Receipt images are NOT stored permanently on your device**
- **Receipt images are NEVER uploaded to our servers**
- Receipt scanning is optional - you can manually enter transactions instead

**Third-party processing:** Receipt images may be sent to OpenAI for text extraction. See "Third-Party Services" below.

### Photo Library Permission

**Why we need it:** To allow you to select existing receipt images from your photo library for scanning.

**How we use it:**
- Only when you choose to select a receipt photo from your gallery
- Selected images are processed the same way as camera-captured receipts
- Images are NOT stored or uploaded

### Biometric Authentication (Face ID/Fingerprint)

**Why we need it:** To secure your financial data with biometric authentication.

**How we use it:**
- Only when you enable biometric lock in settings
- Used to verify your identity before accessing the App
- Biometric data never leaves your device and is handled by your device's operating system

## Third-Party Services

### OpenAI (Receipt Scanning)

When you use the receipt scanning feature:
- Receipt images are sent to OpenAI's Vision API for text extraction
- OpenAI processes the image to identify merchant, amount, date, and items
- OpenAI's data handling is governed by their privacy policy: https://openai.com/privacy
- You can avoid this by manually entering transactions instead

### Google Play Services

The App uses Google Play Services for:
- In-app purchases and subscription management
- App distribution and updates
- Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Data Storage and Security

- **100% Local Storage:** All your financial data is stored only on your device using encrypted local storage (AsyncStorage)
- **No Cloud Sync:** We do not sync your data to any cloud servers
- **No Account Required:** You don't need to create an account or provide an email address
- **Device-Specific:** Your data exists only on the device where you use the App

## Premium Features and Payments

- Premium subscriptions are processed through Google Play Store
- Google handles all payment information - we never see your payment details
- Subscription status is stored locally on your device

## Data Backup

- You can manually create backups and save them to your cloud storage (Google Drive, iCloud)
- Backups are encrypted and stored in your personal cloud account
- We do not have access to your backup files

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:
- **Access:** View all your data within the App
- **Delete:** Clear all data from the App settings or uninstall the App
- **Export:** Export your data as CSV from the App settings

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
